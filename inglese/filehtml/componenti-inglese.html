SYD BARRET

Role: Guitar, vocals, primary composer in the early years.

Contributions: Syd Barrett was one of the founding members and the primary composer in the early years of the band. However, due to mental health issues, he left Pink Floyd in 1968.

ROGER WATERS

Role: Bass, vocals, primary composer after Barrett's departure.

Contributions: After the departure of Syd Barrett, Roger Waters became the primary composer and made significant contributions to the concept and lyrics of subsequent Pink Floyd albums.

RICHARD WRIGHT

Role: Keyboards, vocals

Contributions: Wright was a founding member and contributed both musically and vocally to the band. He was temporarily dismissed during the sessions of 'The Wall' in 1979 but returned as a session musician and later as a full member.

NICK MASON

Role: Drums, percussion.

Contributions: Nick Mason is the only member to be present in all phases of Pink Floyd. He contributed with his drumming skills and participated in the recordings of all the albums

DAVID GILMOUR

Role: Guitar, vocals.

Contributions: Gilmour replaced Syd Barrett in 1968 and became a key member. He brought his distinctive guitar style and contributed vocally, becoming one of the main voices of the band.
